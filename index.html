Research Intern at Niantic Spatial • Multimodal AI / LLMs / Computer Vision

Hi! I am a Research Intern at Niantic Spatial and a PhD Student at the University of Bologna. My research is focused on Multimodal AI, specifically applying Large Language Models (LLMs) and Neural Radiance Fields (NeRFs) to tackle 3D spatial problems.

I have extensive hands-on experience in distributed training and fine-tuning LLMs on large clusters, utilizing tens of GPUs for complex, large-scale models.

I will be finishing my PhD in March 2026 and am exploring future full-time roles.
Feel free to reach out to discuss potential opportunities or collaborations!
